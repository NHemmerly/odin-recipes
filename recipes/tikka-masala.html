Tikka Masala

Description

Tikka masala sauce is an onion and tomato-based gravy popular in much of Indian cuisine. Tikka masala sauce is actually more popular outside of India, playing a large role in restaurant menus around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 diced medium white onions
  • 1 1/2 inch ginger
  • 4-5 large chopped garlic cloves
  • 4 large ripe tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoon tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Indian red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon Coriander powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon honey or sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon kasuri methi
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1/2 - 3/4 cup water
