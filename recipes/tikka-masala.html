Tikka Masala

Description

Tikka masala sauce is an onion and tomato-based gravy popular in much of Indian cuisine. Tikka masala sauce is actually more popular outside of India, playing a large role in restaurant menus around the world.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 diced medium white onions
  • 1 1/2 inch ginger
  • 4-5 large chopped garlic cloves
  • 4 large ripe tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoon tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Indian red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon Coriander powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon honey or sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon kasuri methi
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 1/2 - 3/4 cup water

Instructions

  1. Heat one tablespoon butter and one tablespoon oil in a pan on medium heat. Add one teaspoon cumin seeds and small piece of cinnamon stick.
    Toast the cumin seeds for 30 seconds add 2 chopped medium onion, 1 ½ inch ginger, 4 large garlic cloves and a pinch of salt.
  2. Sautefor 7-8 minutes till onions slightly brown.
  3. Add four large ripe tomatoes with the juices, 2 teaspoon tomato paste, ½ teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon Indian red chili powder, 1 tablespoon coriander powder, 1 teaspoon cumin powder and salt to taste. Mix it well and cook for 12-15 minutes till mixture turns soft and some the water from the tomato is evaporated.
  4. Remove the cinnamon stick and cool the mixture and transfer it to a blender. Make a fine paste.
  5. Now transfer the sauce back to the same pan, add ¼ cup heavy cream, ½ teaspoon honey or sugar, 1 ½ teaspoon dried fenugreek leaves, ½ teaspoon garam masala, 1 tablespoon butter, water to adjust the thickness of the gravy ( about ½ to ¾th cup)
  6. Mix and check for seasoning. Simmer the tikka sauce for 5 minutes and it's done
