Malai Kofta

Description

Malai Kofta is a North Indian paneer dumpling served with a rich sauce. Malai kofta is also often served with rice, naan, roti, or paratha.

Ingredients

  • 1 heaped cup paneer
  • 1 heaped cup boiled and crumbled potato
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons corn flour
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 2 tablespoons coriander leaves
  • Salt as needed
  • 1/2 to 3/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cashews
  • 1 tablespoon raisins (optional)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Boil potatoes until fork tender and not mushy.
  2. Crumble the potatoes and add to a mixing bowl with crumbled paneer, ginger paste, corn flour, salt, garam masala, and chopped coriander leaves.
  3. Add raisins and cashews.
  4. Mix everything well and divide the dough into 8 equal parts. Roll them to balls.
  5. Heat oil in a large pan for deep frying.
  6. Slide the koftas one by one into the hot oil.
  7. Deep fry on medium heat until crisp and golden. Remove and cool on a steel colander or plate.
